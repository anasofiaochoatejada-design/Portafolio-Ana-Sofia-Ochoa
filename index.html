Who am I?

I’m a product designer with the soul of an artist and Colombian roots, which has given me a unique and sensitive perspective on aesthetics, functionality, and cultural diversity. My self-taught background in art has sharpened my eye for detail and visual intuition, while my training in product design has equipped me with the tools to turn ideas into real, responsible, and well-crafted solutions. I create products that not only work, but also respect the environment and connect with people on a deeper level.
