Kids Bench - Rock A Zoo

Rock-a-Zoo is a modular rocking chair for children, inspired by alebrijes. The base is shaped like an animal and comes with interchangeable parts—such as legs, ears, and tails—that allow kids to create their own fantastic creature while playing. It combines a seat, a toy, and a decorative object into a single playful and creative piece. Designed in collaboration with Isabela Stevens and Pablo Pesquería, using Rhino modeling, CNC manufacturing, and workshop craftsmanship
